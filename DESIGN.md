# Design Guidelines — Brew & Co

## Color Palette
- Background: warm off-white / cream
- Foreground: deep espresso brown
- Primary: rich coffee brown (#6F4E37)
- Secondary: warm caramel / tan (#C8A97E)
- Accent: muted gold (#D4A853)
- Muted: soft latte beige (#F5ECD7)
- Destructive: muted red (#B94A48)

## Typography
- Heading Font: Playfair Display (serif, elegant)
- Body Font: Lato (clean, readable)

## Elevation & Spacing
- Generous whitespace between sections
- Subtle card shadows for menu items
- Rounded corners on cards and buttons

## Components
- Full-width hero with background image overlay
- Section dividers using subtle color blocks
- Menu cards with image, title, description, price
- Sticky navigation bar with smooth scroll links
- Footer with contact info and social links
