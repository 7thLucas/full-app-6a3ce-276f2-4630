# Brew & Co

## Overview
Brew & Co is a single-page cafe coffee landing page. It presents the brand, menu highlights, ambiance, and a call to action for visitors to come in or place an order.

## Users
Coffee enthusiasts, locals, and walk-in customers looking for a warm, inviting cafe experience.

## Brand & Tone
- Warm, cozy, artisanal
- Friendly and welcoming
- Premium but approachable

## Core Sections
1. Hero — bold headline, tagline, CTA button
2. About — brief brand story
3. Menu Highlights — featured drinks and food items
4. Gallery / Ambiance — visual showcase
5. Contact / Visit Us — address, hours, map or directions

## Anti-references
- No overly corporate or sterile design
- No cluttered layouts

## Strategic Principles
- Single page, smooth scroll navigation
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Fast and lightweight
