# Brew & Co 123 — Cafe Landing Page

## Product Name
**Brew & Co 123**

## What It Is
A single-page marketing landing website for an independent cafe shop specialising in coffee. Visitors land on one clean page that tells the cafe's story, showcases its menu highlights, and gives them everything they need to visit or get in touch. No multi-page navigation, no e-commerce complexity — one focused, high-quality digital front door.

## Users

### Primary User — The Cafe Customer
Local coffee drinkers discovering the cafe for the first time via search, social media, or a friend's recommendation. They need to quickly understand: *What does this place offer? Where is it? Is it my kind of vibe?*

### Secondary User — The Cafe Owner
The owner who manages their digital presence. They need a page that looks as good as their cafe feels, requires no technical maintenance, and reliably converts curious visitors into paying customers.

## Positioning
Warm, artisanal, and approachable. The landing page mirrors the in-cafe experience — unhurried, quality-focused, and personal. It stands apart from generic business directory listings (Google Maps alone, Facebook page only) by offering a dedicated, beautifully designed space that communicates brand and quality at a glance.

## Tone & Brand
- **Tone**: Warm, welcoming, confident, artisanal
- **Visual character**: Rich earthy tones (deep espresso browns, warm ambers, cream whites), high-quality coffee photography, generous whitespace
- **Voice**: Approachable but not casual — the coffee is taken seriously

## Core Sections (Single Page Structure)
1. **Hero** — Bold headline, tagline, and a striking coffee/cafe visual. Primary CTA ("Find Us" or "See the Menu").
2. **Menu Highlights** — Signature coffee offerings and seasonal specials, presented visually with category filter tabs. Each card shows image, category badge, name, description, and price.
3. **Our Story** — A brief, personal narrative about the cafe's origins, philosophy, and what makes the coffee worth the trip.
4. **Gallery** — Masonry-style image grid showcasing the cafe, the drinks, and the atmosphere (toggled via `showGallerySection` flag in configurables).
5. **Visit Us** — Address, opening hours (structured table), phone, email, and optional map embed (`showMapEmbed` flag).
6. **Contact & Social** — Simple contact prompt and links to social channels (Instagram, Facebook, X).

## Strategic Principles
- **One goal, one page**: every element serves the visitor's decision to walk through the door (or pick up the phone).
- **Speed over features**: fast load, no unnecessary scripts or embeds.
- **Visual quality is the brand**: photography and layout carry the first impression — design quality is non-negotiable.
- **Mobile-first reality**: most cafe discovery happens on phones; the design must feel native on mobile.

## Scope Boundaries
- **In scope**: Hero, menu highlights, story, location/hours, contact CTA — all on a single scrollable page.
- **Out of scope**: Online ordering, table reservations, user accounts, loyalty programmes, blog, multi-language.

## North Star Metric
**Weekly customer enquiries generated** (contact form submissions + "Get Directions" clicks). Direct proxy for new customers the page produces each week. Secondary: estimated new customer value per week (enquiries × visit conversion × average customer spend).
